# Global AI Stewardship — Stakeholder Map

Created: 2026-05-02
Owner: Nea / The Steward
Status: Internal research. No outreach without Avi approval.

This expands the North America map into a global view, prioritising countries most likely to be receptive to voluntary, human-flourishing-centred AI Stewardship. Tiering reflects mission alignment, openness to multilateral partnership, and strength of public-good AI infrastructure already in place.

---

## Tier S — Most Likely To Be On Board First

These countries already publish national AI strategies that explicitly centre human rights, public good, wellbeing, sustainability, or rights-based governance. They are first-circle partners.

### 🇫🇮 Finland
- Mission alignment: extremely high. Published "AI 4.0" with public-good framing; runs *Elements of AI* (free national AI literacy, 1M+ learners globally).
- Bodies: Ministry of Economic Affairs and Employment; Sitra (innovation fund, futures research); University of Helsinki AI lab.
- Hook: wellbeing economics, AI literacy, transparent governance.

### 🇩🇰 Denmark
- Mission alignment: very high. Strong public-trust posture; data-ethics led; circular economy alignment.
- Bodies: Ministry of Industry, Business and Financial Affairs; Danish Data Ethics Council; Digitalisation Agency.

### 🇸🇪 Sweden
- Mission alignment: very high. AI Sweden (national centre); strong public-sector AI ethics frameworks.
- Bodies: AI Sweden; Vinnova (innovation agency); RISE Research Institutes.

### 🇳🇴 Norway
- Mission alignment: very high. Sovereign-wealth model already aligned with universal-dividend thinking; strong digital-rights posture.
- Bodies: Ministry of Digitalisation and Public Governance; Norwegian Board of Technology; NorwAI.

### 🇮🇸 Iceland
- Mission alignment: very high. Small, agile, ecological; strong democratic experimentation history.
- Bodies: Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation; Icelandic Centre for Future Studies.

### 🇳🇿 New Zealand
- Mission alignment: very high. Wellbeing Budget framework; Te Tiriti / Indigenous-data-sovereignty leadership.
- Bodies: Ministry of Business, Innovation & Employment; AI Forum NZ; Te Mana Raraunga (Māori Data Sovereignty Network); Te Hiku Media.

### 🇨🇦 Canada
- Mission alignment: very high. Federal Public Service AI Strategy 2025–27 already uses our principles; G7 GovAI Grand Challenge convening.
- (Detailed in `north-america-stakeholder-map.md`.)

### 🇨🇭 Switzerland
- Mission alignment: very high. Multilateral home (Geneva); strong direct-democracy traditions; AI Council convened 2024.
- Bodies: Federal Office of Communications (OFCOM); SwissCore; Geneva-based UN bodies.

### 🇨🇷 Costa Rica
- Mission alignment: very high. No standing army; constitutional environmental rights; National AI Strategy 2024 emphasising rights and sustainability.
- Bodies: Ministry of Science, Innovation, Technology and Telecommunications (MICITT); INCAE Business School.

### 🇧🇹 Bhutan
- Mission alignment: extremely high (philosophically). Gross National Happiness framework; deeply aligned doctrine.
- Bodies: GNH Centre; Ministry of Information and Communications.

## Tier 1 — Strong Alignment, Major Capacity

### 🇪🇺 European Union
- EU AI Act in force; Council of Europe AI Convention 2024 (first binding international AI treaty).
- Bodies: European AI Office; EU AI Board; European Centre for Algorithmic Transparency (JRC, Seville); Council of Europe (CAI).

### 🇫🇷 France
- Strong public AI investment; hosts UNESCO; Mistral ecosystem; AI Action Summit Paris 2025.
- Bodies: Conseil National du Numérique; Inria; CNIL.

### 🇩🇪 Germany
- AI Made in Europe leadership; strong worker-codetermination culture aligning with autonomy doctrine.
- Bodies: Federal Ministry for Digital and Transport; Plattform Lernende Systeme; HIIG (Humboldt Institute for Internet and Society).

### 🇳🇱 Netherlands
- Public-good AI infrastructure; SURF; strong civic-tech.
- Bodies: Ministry of the Interior; Netherlands AI Coalition; Rathenau Instituut.

### 🇮🇪 Ireland
- AI Strategy emphasising trustworthy AI; CeADAR.

### 🇪🇸 Spain
- Hosts EU AI Office sandbox; AESIA (AI Supervision Agency, Coruña).

### 🇵🇹 Portugal
- INCoDe.2030 digital-skills programme; rights-based posture.

### 🇪🇪 Estonia
- e-Estonia digital-state leadership; X-Road; strong civic infrastructure.

### 🇰🇷 South Korea
- AI Basic Act 2024; strong public R&D; rights protections increasing.
- Bodies: Ministry of Science and ICT; KISA; AI Safety Institute (Seoul, 2024).

### 🇯🇵 Japan
- Hiroshima AI Process leadership; Society 5.0 framing aligns with human flourishing.
- Bodies: Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry (METI); AI Safety Institute Japan; Cabinet Office.

### 🇸🇬 Singapore
- Model AI Governance Framework (mature, voluntary); IMDA / AI Verify Foundation.
- Bodies: IMDA; AI Verify Foundation; Centre for AI and Data Governance (SMU).

### 🇬🇧 United Kingdom
- AI Safety Institute (now AI Security Institute); rights and standards posture.
- Bodies: AISI; Ada Lovelace Institute; Alan Turing Institute; CDEI.

### 🇦🇺 Australia
- AI Ethics Framework; emerging AI Act work; strong Indigenous-data-sovereignty advocacy.
- Bodies: Department of Industry, Science and Resources; CSIRO Data61; Maiam nayri Wingara (Indigenous Data Sovereignty).

### 🇺🇾 Uruguay
- AGESIC digital state; rights-led posture; #1 in Latin America on AI government readiness.

### 🇨🇱 Chile
- National AI Policy emphasising ethics and inclusion; CENIA AI centre.

## Tier X — Avoid first-wave outreach

- Active conflict states.
- Authoritarian regimes with documented surveillance use of AI.
- Sanctioned governments.
- Bodies whose mandate is offensive military AI development.
