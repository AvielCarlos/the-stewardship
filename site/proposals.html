How signal becomes outcome

  1. Open — discussion in the open for at least 72 hours.
  2. Refine — proposers may iterate; counter-proposals welcome.
  3. Vote — once moved to state:vote, a window opens (5–21 days depending on type).
  4. Decide — outcome and weights recorded in the public log; reversible.
