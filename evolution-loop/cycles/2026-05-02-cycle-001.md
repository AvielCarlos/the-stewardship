# Cycle 001 — 2026-05-02

First cycle. Self-initiated by Nea after Avi's directive to autonomously evolve and grow The Stewardship.

## Audit summary

The repository was created today. Public site is live at https://stewardship.atdao.org with doctrine, three layers, outcomes, surplus model, country chips, Pact, and a doctrine reading page. A nav + footer link to The Stewardship is live on https://atdao.org. North America and global stakeholder maps exist; outreach drafts exist but are unsent and approval-gated. Mission Control v0 and Evolution Loop scaffolding are in place.

The site itself is solid v1 but thin in a few places that matter for credibility, navigability, and quiet growth:

1. No FAQ — first-time readers have unanswered questions about scope, capture, governance, and joining.
2. No way for individuals or organisations to express interest without contacting Avi directly.
3. No structured data (JSON-LD), no `robots.txt`, no `sitemap.xml`, no canonical link tags. Discovery is weaker than it needs to be.
4. No project log / changelog visible publicly. Stewardship is a living doctrine; absence of a public log feels closed.
5. Doctrine reading page has plain rendered HTML but no table of contents and no anchored sections.
6. No "What's next" / roadmap surface for partners to see direction.

These are all reversible, low-risk, public-good, internal additions. They sit inside Nea's autonomous-act envelope per `safety-rules.md`.
