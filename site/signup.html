Voluntary · Transparent · Revocable

Become a member

Help steward an intelligence that serves life.

Membership in The Stewardship is voluntary, light, and reversible. Phase 0 is intentionally simple: tell us who you are, optionally where you are, and optionally what kind of stewardship you care about most. We do not collect more than that.

When verified-member voting opens (Phase 2), members may participate in proposals, votes, and treasury decisions. Until then, signing up just keeps you in the loop and gives you early-member status when we're ready.

Sign up — three lightweight options

Email

Quietly join the list

Email a one-line signup with your handle and (optionally) where you are and what kind of stewardship matters to you.

GitHub

Open a member intro

Open a "member intro" issue on GitHub. Public, transparent, and easy to verify.

Pact

Sign the Pact

Once Phase 2 opens, you'll be invited to sign The Pact and gain verified member status. Until then, your intro counts.

What we ask for

  • A handle (your real name or a chosen one — your call).
  • Optional: a region or city, so local stewardship circles can find you.
  • Optional: what kind of stewardship you care most about (personal, community, civilisational, ecological, indigenous, etc.).

What we never ask for

  • Your wallet, your ID, your social, your phone, your address.
  • Your political affiliation.
  • Your money.

Your rights, from day one

  • Quiet exit — leave anytime, no questions, no records preserved beyond what you've publicly published.
  • Privacy — your data is treated as a sacred trust. Minimised. Never sold.
  • Voice — once Phase 2 opens, you vote on what matters.
  • Transparency — every proposal, vote, and expenditure is in the open.
